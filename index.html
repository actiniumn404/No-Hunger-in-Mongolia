Statistics

35% of Mongolians are malnourished

68% of people in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia's capital where 45% of Mongolia lives, has proper access to food

20% of Mongolian children under 5 years old are anemic (which is caused by a lack of food), and 65% of which are undernourished

Many Mongolians lack a high amount of Vitamin D

Many Mongolians lack iron. This is caused by an insufficient diet
